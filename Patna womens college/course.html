Course

Bachelor of Business Administration [BBA] {Hons.}

BBA (Hons) is an undergraduate course that pertains to business administration. It is a 3-year course that is pursued by candidates after completion of their 10+2 level or equivalent education. Few colleges also offer this course for a duration of 4-years. The course is divided into 6 semesters. Each year of study comprises 2 semesters.

Eligibility for Admission

A citizen of India who has passed any Intermediate examination of Bihar University or any University in India or aboard recognized as equivalent through 10+2 pattern of education obtaining 50% of marks in aggregate of all the subjects.

About Us

Patna Women's College (PWC), Patna was established by Bishop B.J. Sullivan, S.J., Bishop of Patna and Mother M. Josephine A.C. Superior General of the Apostolic Carmel in the year 1940. The campus is spread over 5.2 acres of land. PWC is approved and granted by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and University Grants Commission (UGC) respectively. The College offers undergraduate (BA (Hons), B.Com (Hons) and B.Sc (Hons), B.Ed), postgraduate (MCA, MA, M.Sc) courses in various domains. PWC is affiliated to Patna University.
